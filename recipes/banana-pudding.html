Banana Pudding

Description:

A rich creamy vanilla pudding with a burst of flavor from the sliced up bananas and a hint of crunch from the vanilla wafers. Topped with fluffy coolwhip. This is a tasty delight for any gathering that will keep you wanting more!

Ingredients

  • 1 to 1 1/2 bananas per box of pudding
  • Jello brand instant vanilla pudding
  • Typically half a box of vanilla wafers (add as perferred).
  • 1 8oz container of Cool Whip
  • Milk (for pudding).

Steps

  1. Make pudding is instructed on vanilla pudding box.Add to bowl.
  2. Slice bananas to your liking, add to bowl.
  3. Add half a box of vanilla wafers to bowl.
  4. Mix thoroughly.Ensure the wafers are covered with pudding.
  5. Place in fridge for 30 minutes to an hour depending on how soft you want the wafers.
  6. Remove from fridge.
  7. Top with coolwhip and any other toppings as desired.
  8. Thats it! Enjoy!
